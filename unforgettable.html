My Most Unforgettable Experience

This photo was taken at Daranak Falls at Tanay,Rizal. This trip was unplanned. Our original plan was to purchase a ticket at a music festival and watch it but unfortunately, the ticket was sold out quickly and we unable to purchase any and it was still early in the morning. For our trip not to be wasted, we decided to plan a trip for us to go swimming. As they say, unplanned trip was the best, I will always remember this trip I made with my friends. We didn't even have any spare clothes and underwear that time, so we only purchase cheap underwears for us at a store near the terminal.
